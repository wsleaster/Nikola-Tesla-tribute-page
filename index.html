facts about Nikola tesla

  • 10JUL1856Nikola Tesla was born in Smijan, Austrian empire (modern day Croatia) to father Milutin Tesla and mother ƒêuka Tesla
  • 1875 Nikola Tesla enrolled in Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria where he never graduated due to gambling and the loss of his father
  • 1882 Nikola Tesla moved to france to work for Thomas Edison's company, latter moving to New York in 1884. His work was in Thomas Edison's dirrect current generators which he found inefficient
  • 1886after telling Edison he could design a better generator Edison offered him 50 thousand as a challenge. Upon revealing the new generator Edison instead wanted to give him 10$ raise saying "Tesla, you don't understand our American humor." Tesla immediately resigned
  • 1886, post EdisonNikola Tesla founded the Tesla Electric Light & Manufacturing with electrical arc based lighting. There was little interest and he eventually lost his patents, which were tied to his company and he went under.
  • April 1887Tikola Tesla founded the Tesla Electric Company where he invented an induction motor running on AC(alternating current) beggining the war of the Currents, AC verus DC.
  • 7JAN1943 Nikola Tesla died alone from coronary thrombosis. He had no children and his estate was taken to Manhattan Storage and Warehouse Company. He died with around 300 patents to his name, most of them in America but he did have patents all over the world.
